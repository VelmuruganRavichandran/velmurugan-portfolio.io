About Me

Hello! I'm Velmurugan, a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Data Science from The American College, Madurai Kamaraj University. Throughout my academic journey, I have built a strong foundation in statistical analysis, machine learning techniques, and programming languages, particularly Python. Though I am a fresher in the industry, my academic projects, including Online Food Order Prediction and Used Car Price Prediction, have equipped me with essential skills in Data Analysis and Model Building. Recently, I completed a internship at Softlogic Systems, where I worked on a range of projects such as Diabetes Prediction, Hotel Room Price Prediction, Sentimental Analysis, Image Classification, and Helmet Detection. These experiences have provided me with practical knowledge in Data Cleaning, Data Visualization, Model evaluation. I'm a self-driven learner, having completed a Data Science Bootcamp on Udemy, attended a National Level Workshop on Cyber Security, and earned a certification in Data Analytics and Visualization from Accenture through Forage. I am eager to kickstart my career in Data Science, where I can apply my skills and continue to grow in this exciting field.
